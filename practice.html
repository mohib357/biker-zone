Safety First! Always obey the rules of the road. Obey all traffic signals, signs, and laws. Get in the mindset of “driving” your bike—not just “riding” your bike. This will help you be a more focused and legally compliant bike rider.

If your helmet is more than 5 years old, has been in a crash, or has visible cracks and damage, it's time to replace it.

Before riding, check the brakes, tires, and chain. Ensure the bike is properly lubricated and test ride it for safety.
